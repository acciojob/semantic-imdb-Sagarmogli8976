Summary

3 Idiots is a story about three engineering students who challenge the traditional education system. The film focuses on friendship, innovation, and the importance of understanding concepts instead of rote learning.

Actors

The film stars Aamir Khan as Rancho, R. Madhavan as Farhan, and Sharman Joshi as Raju. Their performances make the movie both emotional and inspiring.

Favorite Quote

"All is well." This dialogue teaches us to stay positive and confident even during difficult times.
